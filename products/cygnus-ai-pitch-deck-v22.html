The Problem

Enterprise documents are massive, messy,
and mission-critical.

Manual and legacy automated processing breaks down exactly where it matters most.

Documents at impossible scale

Real-world submissions arrive as bundles of hundreds — even thousands — of pages, mixing invoices, manifests, permits, and forms in one file.

Scans, not clean PDFs

A large share of enterprise documents are scanned, skewed, or low quality. Most AI tools degrade badly on anything that isn't digital-native.

Errors carry real cost

In regulated, high-volume operations, a hallucinated field or a misclassified page means compliance risk, delays, and rework.

What Makes Cygnus-AI Different

Intelligence at the page level.

Page-Level Classification

Every single page is classified individually — so a 900-page bundle of mixed document types is automatically organized into its components, with nothing missed.

Continuity Check & Page Linking

Cygnus-AI detects when pages belong together — linking all pages of the same invoice across a large bundle and verifying nothing is out of order or missing.

No Hallucination

Extraction is grounded strictly in document content. If the data isn't on the page, Cygnus-AI doesn't invent it — a non-negotiable for compliance workflows.

These capabilities work together: classify each page → link related pages → extract with verified continuity.

Built for Real-World Documents

If your team can read it,
Cygnus-AI can process it.

  • 1000+ page documents — no page limits forcing manual file splits.
  • Scanned & handwritten — robust on photocopies, faxes, low-quality scans, and handwriting.
  • Any orientation & rotation — upside-down, sideways, or skewed pages handled automatically.
  • Any language — multilingual documents, no separate configuration.
  • Auto de-duplication — duplicate pages and resubmissions detected and filtered.
  • Mixed document bundles — invoices, packing lists, certificates, and forms in one upload.
  • Grounded extraction — every output traceable to the source page.
Example

One upload. Complete clarity.

A single 1,200-page scanned bundle arrives — some pages rotated, some handwritten, several languages mixed in. Cygnus-AI classifies every page, filters the duplicates, groups the 87 invoices it contains, links each invoice's pages together, flags one with a missing page — and extracts structured data from all of them. Automatically.
