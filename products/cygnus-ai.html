Core Intelligence

Intelligence at the page level.

Three capabilities that work together — classify, link, then extract — making Cygnus-AI fundamentally different from document tools that operate on the whole file.

Page-Level Classification

Every single page is classified individually — so a 900-page bundle of mixed document types is automatically organized into its components, with nothing missed or misrouted.

Continuity Check & Page Linking

Cygnus-AI detects when pages belong together — linking all pages of the same invoice across a large bundle and verifying nothing is out of order or missing before extraction begins.

Zero Hallucination

Extraction is grounded strictly in document content. If the data isn't on the page, Cygnus-AI doesn't invent it — a non-negotiable for compliance workflows where accuracy is audited.

→ Classify each page  ·  Link related pages  ·  Extract with verified continuity — these three steps run together across every document, every time.
